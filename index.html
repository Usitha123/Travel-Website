Mirissa

Mirissa, a tranquil coastal town in Sri Lanka, is famed for its stunning sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife, and whale watching tours. It offers excellent surfing, snorkeling, and seafood dining. Mirissa's picturesque Coconut Tree Hill and serene atmosphere make it a popular destination for relaxation and adventure.

Galle

Galle, a coastal city in Sri Lanka, is renowned for its well-preserved 16th-century Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It features colonial architecture, charming streets, and vibrant markets. Galle also offers beautiful beaches, a historic lighthouse, and a rich cultural heritage blending Portuguese, Dutch, and British influences.

Ella

Ella, a picturesque town in Sri Lanka's Uva Province, is known for its lush landscapes, scenic hikes like Ella Rock and Little Adam's Peak, and iconic sites such as the Nine Arches Bridge and Ravana Falls. It offers tea plantation tours, cultural experiences, and stunning viewpoints.
